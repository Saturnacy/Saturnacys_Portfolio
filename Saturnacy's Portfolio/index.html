About Me

I am João Pedro Pessôa Primo, but online, you probably know me as Saturnacy. Welcome to my webpage — I hope you enjoy your stay!

Ever since I was a kid, I've been fascinated by computers and their ability to create entire worlds and universes. That curiosity quickly turned into a passion for the science and development behind these incredible machines. Today, I'm pursuing a Computer Science degree at CESAR School, turning that passion into a career.

Interests

I absolutely love video games — they've shaped who I am in more ways than I can describe. My mission is to share that love with the world and help others see the beauty in interactive experiences. If I had to choose a favorite, it would be the timeless sandbox of Minecraft, a game that never stops inspiring creativity.

When I'm not coding or gaming, you'll probably find me indulging in my other hobbies: enjoying good food (especially popcorn), exploring water parks, diving into historical conversations, or simply observing the wonderful wildlife on this planet.

Fun Fact

I'm not actually from Saturn (I just moved there). I was born and raised on Earth — specifically in the South American country known as Brazil.
